2026 FIFA WORLD CUP QUALIFIERS

As of now, a total of 48 countries will ultimately compete in the fifa world cup. that's a fixed number of teams in the tournament's expanded format.

However, not all 48 teams have been confirmed yet:

  • 42 teams have already qualified so far
  • The remaining 6 places will be decided through playoff matches (including European and inter-continental playoffs) scheduled for march 2026

So, right now;

  • 42 teams have qualified
  • 48 teams will be in the tournament once qualification is complete
